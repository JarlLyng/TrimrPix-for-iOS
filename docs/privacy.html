Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

TrimrPix for iOS is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how TrimrPix handles your data.

The short version: TrimrPix does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photo compression happens entirely on your device.

Data collection

TrimrPix does not collect personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or user tracking of any kind.

The only data transmitted from the app is anonymous crash reports via Sentry, which include:

  • Crash stack traces (technical error information)
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App version

Crash reports do not contain photos, personal identifiers, Apple ID, email addresses, or any information that can be linked to your identity.

Photo access

TrimrPix accesses only the photos you explicitly select through Apple's photo picker. The app requires write access to your Photos library in order to replace originals with compressed versions.

Your photos are processed entirely on-device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

Data storage

TrimrPix does not store your photos or image data. Compression happens on-device and the result is saved directly back to your Photos library. Any temporary working file is deleted as soon as the photo is saved — the app keeps no lasting copies.

App preferences (quality level, target size, and metadata settings) and a running total of the space you have saved are stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms. This data is never transmitted.

Third-party services

Sentry — used for crash reporting and app stability monitoring. Sentry receives anonymous crash data only. No personal data is shared. Sentry's privacy policy.

Umami — privacy-friendly analytics used on this website only (not in the app). It records anonymous, aggregate visit data such as page views, referrer, country and browser. It sets no cookies, performs no cross-site tracking, and collects no personal data. About Umami.

Beyond these, TrimrPix uses no third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect or process personal data.

Children's privacy

TrimrPix does not collect any personal data from any user, including children. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal information to function.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out at iamjarl.com.
