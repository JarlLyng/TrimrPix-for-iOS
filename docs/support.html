Troubleshooting

"Photos access denied" — TrimrPix needs write access to your Photos library to replace originals with compressed versions. Go to Settings > TrimrPix and enable full Photos access.

"Photo not found in library" — This can happen if a photo was deleted after you selected it. Try selecting your photos again.

Compression seems slow — Large photos or iCloud photos that need to be downloaded first can take a bit longer. The progress indicator shows which photo is being processed.
