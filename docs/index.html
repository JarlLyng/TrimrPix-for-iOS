Metadata control

Keep date and time, strip GPS location, remove camera data — you decide what stays.

📊

Savings estimate

See exactly how much space you'll save before committing to compression.

In-place replacement

Originals are replaced directly — no duplicates cluttering your library.

iCloud compatible

Works with photos stored in iCloud. They're downloaded, compressed, and saved back automatically.
